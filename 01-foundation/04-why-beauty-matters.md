# Why Beauty Matters

Beauty in Ontahí is not decoration.

It is a way of teaching attention.

The goal is not to make documentation prettier. The goal is for a developer to spend time inside Ontahí and leave with a sharper sense of structure, rhythm, proportion and language.

Software architecture is full of invisible categories. Beauty makes some of those categories perceptible.

Beauty is a form of care for future engineers.
