# Why Beauty Matters

\term{Beauty} in \concept{Ontahí} is not decoration.

It is a way of teaching attention.

The goal is not to make documentation prettier. The goal is for a developer to spend time inside \concept{Ontahí} and leave with a sharper sense of \term{structure}, \term{rhythm}, proportion and language.

Software architecture is full of invisible \term{categories}. \term{Beauty} makes some of those categories perceptible.

\term{Beauty} is a form of care for future engineers.
