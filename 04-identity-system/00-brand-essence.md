# Identity Principle

Ontahí should feel:

- calm
- precise
- organic
- scholarly
- editorial
- timeless
- warm
- serious without being cold

It should avoid feeling:

- corporate
- blockchain-like
- AI-hype
- cyberpunk
- startup-generic
- dashboard-heavy
- visually noisy

The identity system should be quiet enough for books and precise enough for code.

The logo is not the endpoint. It is one expression of the language.
