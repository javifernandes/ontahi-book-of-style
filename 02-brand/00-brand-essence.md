# Brand Essence

Ontahí should feel:

- calm
- precise
- organic
- scholarly
- editorial
- timeless
- warm
- serious without being cold

It should avoid feeling:

- corporate
- blockchain-like
- AI-hype
- cyberpunk
- startup-generic
- dashboard-heavy
- visually noisy
